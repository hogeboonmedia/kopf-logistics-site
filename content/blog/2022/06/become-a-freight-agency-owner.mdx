<h4>What is a Freight Agent?</h4>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A Freight Agent </span><b>connects shippers and carriers</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Like a music agents finds and organize gigs for their clients, freight agents organize jobs for truckers. They are the ones connecting loads to trucks and trucks to loads. Agents source clients, negotiate fees, and plan pickups and deliveries</span></p>

<h4>Independent Freight Agent</h4>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">An Independent Freight Agent is an independent contractor who partners with a third-party logistics company and works under their brokerage license to arrange the transportation of freight.  </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Freight Agents work directly with customers as liaisons and sales agents.  You will be responsible for sourcing contract carriers, negotiating rates, scheduling freight and solving problems quickly and effectively. Your ability to find innovative solutions, negotiate deals and manage day-to-day challenges means financial freedom and a life well-lived.  With your knowledge of the transportation industry and Kopf’s support team, you’ll be on your way to independence and strong earnings!</span></p>
