<p>It is estimated the shipping and logistics industry will be valued at <a href="https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/logistics-market-size-to-reach-usd-12-975-64-billion-by-2027-registering-a-cagr-of-6-5-----valuates-reports-301456137.html">over 12 billion dollars</a> by 2027. With the industry growing rapidly, now might be the perfect time to make the switch and start looking into careers in these fields. If you are a detailed oriented person with a knack for negotiation, this might just be the perfect field for you.<span class="Apple-converted-space">  </span>Keep reading to discover if a career in shipping and logistics is right for you.</p>
<h4><b>1. It’s Big Business</b></h4>
<p>While you may not have heard about jobs in shipping and logistics before now, the industry is booming. The logistics industry alone makes up <a href="https://www.abr.com/interesting-facts-about-the-logistics-industry/">12% of the global GDP</a>, so it’s the perfect time to enter the field.<span class="Apple-converted-space">  </span>Just think about every package or delivery you receive; now imagine how many packages get delivered nationwide each day. There is a massive amount of work that goes into ensuring these packages/deliveries reach you quickly and safely.</p>
<h4><b>2. It’s A High-Pressure Career</b></h4>
<p>The industry is booming, which means a lot of people will depend on you to do your job quickly and efficiently. Your planning and negotiation skills will be what allows those around you to do their jobs.<span class="Apple-converted-space">  </span>Even then, problems will pop up along the way that throws a wrench into your well-thought-out plans.<span class="Apple-converted-space">  </span>Because this field is so varied, you won&#8217;t be doing the same thing every day, so knowing how to think on your feet, keep calm under pressure, and communicate with all parties will be a crucial skill you&#8217;ll need to master.</p>
<h4><b>3. Big Picture Thinking is Necessary</b></h4>
<p>To be successful in this industry, you&#8217;ll need to be able to see the big picture. You&#8217;ll oversee various aspects of the shipping process, so it’s necessary to understand it.<span class="Apple-converted-space">  </span>Training and education is important and should be considered. With the field constantly growing, you&#8217;ll need to stay on top of the newest processes as well as operations and management techniques. By keeping the big picture in mind, you&#8217;ll be able to spot areas where additional training is needed.</p>
