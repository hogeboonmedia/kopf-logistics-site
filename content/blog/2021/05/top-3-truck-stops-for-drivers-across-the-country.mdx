<p>When you’re on the road for hours or days at a time, it’s helpful to stop at a place you can consider a home away from home for a while.  There are numerous truck stops across the United States that do have top-quality amenities for weary drivers. From private showers and jacuzzis to barbershops and movie theaters, the best truck stops in America are waiting for you to stop and relax!</p>



<p><strong>Here are three truck stops across the country Kopf Drivers consistently enjoy.</strong></p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Truck Stop 1: </strong><a href="https://iowa80truckstop.com/"><strong>Iowa 80 Truck Stop in Walcott, Iowa</strong></a></h2>



<p>The Iowa 80 Truck Stop can easily start to feel more like a resort than a simple rest stop. Before even considering the amenities, Drivers can enjoy the surrounding attractions, including the <a href="http://iowa80truckingmuseum.com/">Iowa 80 Trucking Museum</a>, housing over 100 antique trucks. It’s the perfect stop for classic rig enthusiasts!</p>



<p>When you’re ready for a little rest and relaxation, take advantage of this truck stop’s amenities by starting with a good meal. Looking for fast food? 24/7 food options include Wendy’s, Chester’s Chicken, Pizza Hut, Taco Bell, and more. Craving comfort food? If you’re craving the comforts of home, don’t forget to stop by the Iowa 80 Kitchen for homestyle cooking.</p>



<p>The options are endless when it comes to what you can experience at the Iowa 80 Truck Stop. Some of their unique amenities include:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Barbershop</li>
<li>Chiropractor</li>
<li>Dental office</li>
<li>Dog wash</li>
<li>Library</li>
<li>Movie theater</li>
<li>Workout room</li>
<li>…and more!</li>
</ul>



<p>As you might imagine, this truck stop is a pretty popular place with truckers looking for a little luxury. Fortunately, with over 900 parking spots, there is always room for you at the Iowa 80 Truck Stop!</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Truck Stop 2: </strong><a href="https://www.clearwatertravelplaza.com/"><strong>Clearwater Travel Plaza in Clearwater, Minnesota</strong></a></h2>



<div class="wp-block-media-text alignwide is-stacked-on-mobile" style="grid-template-columns: 22% auto;">
<figure class="wp-block-media-text__media"></figure>
<div class="wp-block-media-text__content">
<p>Let’s face it, living on the road doesn’t always give you many gourmet food options to consider. Most truck stops and travel centers offer fast food. While meals on the go are convenient, treating yourself to something special once in a while can really help energize you for your next drive. </p>



<p>Clearwater Travel Plaza is the perfect place to indulge. Their slogan is: “A touch of home when you’re away from yours. Food that’s comforting and contemporary.”  The Travel Plaza’s dining options consist of a restaurant, pub, bakery, and food court. The Nelson Brothers restaurant is a dream come true for food lovers. In fact, their chicken and wild rice soup is so famous, it landed a segment on Food Network!</p>



<p>A unique feature is their nostalgic Nelson Brothers Bakery.  This hometown bakery is open 24/7. You can take a freshly-made baked good with you as you go! Other unique options include their take and bake pizzas and gourmet sandwich bar.  Be sure to <a href="https://www.clearwatertravelplaza.com/specials-coupons?hsCtaTracking=48b72aa7-d42e-48fc-b9c3-ea5a9fe016a3%7C9e6c53df-c5cd-409c-8e7d-b89c6069a061">visit their website</a> for daily specials and coupons. </p>



<p>Before you hit the road again, you can also take advantage of their full-bathroom showers which can make you feel a bit more like you’re home. While the Clearwater Travel Plaza may not have the craziest amenities in the country, they may just have the most delicious ones.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Truck Stop 3: </strong><a href="http://jubitz.com/"><strong>Jubitz Truck Stop in Portland, Oregon</strong></a></h2>



<p>If you really want to feel like a king at your next rest stop, head to Portland, Oregon. There, you’ll find the Jubitz Truck Stop. This stop has been in business since 1952 and its history of superior hospitality shows. You may never want to leave! </p>



<p>Enjoy a little old-fashioned pampering at the barbershop.  Relax for a few hours by catching the latest movie at their newly renovated cinema with stadium seating and surround sound. Unique dining experiences are available 24/7 at Cascade Grille while the Ponderosa Lounge &amp; Grille features live country western music on the weekends.  You may want to take advantage of their most indulgent amenity and soak in a luxurious private Jacuzzi.  </p>



<p>Some additional unique amenities at Jubitz include: </p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Self-serve dog wash</li>
<li>Fully tiled private showers</li>
<li>Postal Place PDX &#8211; offers a variety of services including notary, copying, money orders, fingerprinting, and more</li>
<li>CleanFleet &#8211; offers 24/7 on-site certified medical examiners</li>
<li>Hotel rooms at Portland Inn</li>
</ul>



<p>With 250 free parking spots on its 15 acres of full-lit parking, Jubitz is considered to have the largest truck parking lot in the Northwest.  Simply put, it’s hard to beat the Jubitz Truck Stop! It has all the amenities you would expect from a classic truck stop, with a modern flare.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>What to Look for in a Truck Stop</strong></h2>



<p>There are many more truck stops and rest areas across the country that could easily make this list. If you are on a new route or just want to find a new place to rest, explore <a href="https://truckerpath.com/">the Trucker Path app.</a> It’s a great tool to find top truck stops rated by Truck Drivers across the US. </p>



<p>Keep in mind, there are some things you can look for in a truck stop to make it feel a bit more comfortable. Some amenities to look for include:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Extra parking spaces</li>
<li>Cheaper fuel prices</li>
<li>Adequate lighting and security</li>
<li>Clean and private bathrooms</li>
<li>A variety of food options</li>
</ul>

<p>Every Driver has a favorite rest stop or two across the country.  If you are near Walcott, Clearwater, or Portland, you may enjoy the Iowa 80 Truck Stop, Clearwater Travel Plaza, and Jubitz Truck Stop. Taking in a few of these top truck stops when you’re on the road can make your <a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/carriers/">next delivery</a> feel more relaxing.  Enjoy your adventures and share your favorite stops with us in the comments below!</p>
