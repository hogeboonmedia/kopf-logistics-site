<h2><b>Top 6 Truck Driver Facts</b></h2>
<p><em>Did you know…</em></p>
<ol>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Number &#8211;</strong> There are more than 3.5 million Truck Drivers in the U.S., showcasing the growth of the industry </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(</span><a href="https://www.census.gov/library/stories/2019/06/america-keeps-on-trucking.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Census Bureau</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">). </span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Age &#8211;</strong> The </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">average age of a truck driver</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> is 46, reflecting the wisdom and experience required in this field </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(</span><a href="https://www.census.gov/library/stories/2019/06/america-keeps-on-trucking.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Census Bureau</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">). </span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><strong>Gender &#8211; </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Most Drivers are men, but the number of women Drivers is on the rise, making up about 14% of Over-the-Road Drivers (</span><a href="https://www.cnbc.com/2023/03/25/women-truck-driving-shortage.html#:~:text=Women%20made%20up%20almost%2014,safety%20roles%20both%20top%2040%25." target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span style="font-weight: 400;">CNBC</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">).</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Pets &#8211;</strong> This fun fact </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">about truck</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> drivers reveals that 60% own pets, and 40% travel with them. <a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/truck-driver-pets/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Read about the best pets for Drivers! </a></span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><strong>Salary &#8211; </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">The average annual salary for a Truck Driver in the U.S. is $91,614 </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">(</span><a href="https://www.indeed.com/career/truck-driver/salaries" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Indeed</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">).</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Mileage &#8211;</strong> </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Truck driving facts</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> like the 327.48 billion miles traveled by all registered trucks in the U.S. in 2021 reveals the vast scope of the industry (</span><a href="https://www.trucking.org/economics-and-industry-data#:~:text=Mileage%3A,by%20combination%20trucks%20in%202021." target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span style="font-weight: 400;">American Trucking Associations</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">).</span></li>
</ol>

<h2><b>TOP 6 TRACTOR-TRAILER </b><b>TRUCK FACTS</b></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Did you know…</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>History &#8211;</strong> The </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">truck facts history</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> begins with the invention of the first semi-trailer truck in 1898 by Alexander Winton in Cleveland, Ohio </span><a href="https://intrans.iastate.edu/news/origins-of-the-semi-trailer-truck/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">(Institute for Transportation</a>).</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Cost &#8211;</strong> The average new truck cab costs $80,000–$200,000, with trailers typically costing $30,000–$80,000 </span>(<a href="https://www.costowl.com/automotive/auto-semi-truck-new-cost.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cost Owl</a>).</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Fuel &#8211;</strong> Delving into semi-</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">truck facts</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">, typical ones in the U.S. hold about 120 gallons, with some storing up to 150 gallons </span>(<a href="https://www.freightwaves.com/news/how-many-gallons-does-it-take-to-fill-up-a-big-rig#:~:text=Most%20big%20rigs%20in%20the,two%20tanks%20for%20longer%20ones." target="_blank" rel="noopener">FreightWaves</a>).</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Engines &#8211;</strong> Truck engines are built to last, with tractor-trailer engines designed to endure up to one million miles </span>(<a href="https://bigrigpros.com/why-do-semi-trucks-last-so-long" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Big Rig Pros</a>)<span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Pounds &#8211;</strong> The average tractor-trailer can carry 80,000 pounds of freight </span>(<a href="https://www.freightwaves.com/news/how-much-weight-can-a-big-rig-carry" target="_blank" rel="noopener">FreightWaves</a>)<span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><strong>Total Freight &#8211; </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Truck facts</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> like the $940.8 billion of gross freight revenue moved in North America in 2022 highlight the significance of the industry (</span><a href="https://www.trucking.org/economics-and-industry-data#:~:text=Tonnage%3A,of%20total%20domestic%20tonnage%20shipped." target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span style="font-weight: 400;">American Trucking Association</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">).</span></li>
</ul>
