<p class="p2"><b>Get Your Documentation in Order</b></p>
<p class="p1">Before getting started with your career in freight and shipping, there are several administrative tasks to complete. First, you&#8217;ll need a registered business and a copy of your state domiciled business registration documents. Most freight agents&#8217; businesses are registered as <a href="https://www.legalzoom.com/articles/difference-between-llc-and-inc"><span class="s1">LLC or INC</span></a>.</p>
<p class="p1">Next, you&#8217;ll need to know your federal tax identification number.</p>
<p class="p1">Finally, to carry out your work as an Independent Freight Agent, you&#8217;ll need a computer with access to a strong internet connection. You&#8217;ll also need access to Microsoft Office products, including Word and Excel.</p>
