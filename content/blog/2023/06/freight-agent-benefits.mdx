<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The transportation industry is thriving and now is the time to enter this growing field. One of the most lucrative opportunities for those looking to start a career in transportation, freight, or logistics is as an Independent Freight Agent. From working remotely to earning high commissions, it’s a career worth checking out. Let’s explore five benefits.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">#1 &#8211; Minimal Start-Up Costs</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">It isn’t expensive to </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2022/06/become-a-freight-agency-owner/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">become a freight agency owner</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. All you’ll need is a laptop or computer, internet, and phone. That’s it. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">While those are the minimum essentials, we would also recommend you invest in training if you are completely new to the industry. At Kopf, we highly recommend </span><a href="https://www.freightbrokerbootcamp.com/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">this affordable program.</span></a></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In addition, you will need a few essential resources. However, as a Freight Agent working under the authority of a Freight Broker, you will not need to purchase them. This is a huge benefit to Agents. As an Agent, the brokerage provides licensing and registration, surety bond, business insurance, Transportation Management System (TMS) software, and more. It’s a key </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2019/10/agent-vs-broker/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">difference between an Agent and Broker</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">#2 &#8211; Work Remotely</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A second benefit is Freight Agents can work anywhere they have strong internet access and cell phone reception. Working remotely has become an attractive benefit for many who want a flexible lifestyle. In addition to flexibility, you’ll also likely save time and expenses you would otherwise spend on things like commuting and wardrobe.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If working remotely is important to you, be sure to research Freight Brokers offering this benefit. Some brokerages have in-house Agents, others offer a hybrid option of a physical location with the ability to work remotely, and yet others are 100% remote. At Kopf, our Independent Freight Agents work remotely.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">#3 &#8211; Be Your Own Boss</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As an Independent Freight Agent, you are not an employee. You are a 1099 independent contractor, which means you are your own boss. For people who like autonomy and thrive without micromanagement, this is a wonderful perk. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As your own boss, you are not required to perform to a certain set of expectations, other than the </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2020/08/freight-agent-goal-setting/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">goals you set</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> for yourself. You don’t have to waste time in unnecessary meetings because you schedule the meetings you want. And the list goes on.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">#4 &#8211; Set Your Own Schedule</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Another benefit Freight Agents enjoy is setting their own schedule. You can work when it’s best for you and your customers. This sets many Agents up for a better </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2021/01/freight-agents-balance-work-home-life/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">work-life balance</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. When you set your own schedule, you can dedicate time to your most important relationships, and plan time for what energizes you. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">It is important to note that setting your own schedule can also lead to overwork. If you’re not careful, you can work 60+ hours a week. So, you must be diligent to set a schedule and stick to it. Find a scheduling tool that works best for you and invite an accountability partner to help keep you on track.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">#5 &#8211; Earn High Commissions</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Lastly, one of the most popular benefits of being a Freight Agent is the potential to earn a high income. New Independent Freight Agents frequently ask how much money they can expect to earn as an agency owner. The great news is the earning potential for talented Freight Agents is truly unlimited. Financial freedom makes this career worth a closer look. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">At Kopf, our Agents earn an above-industry standard 70/30 commission split, and their earnings are not capped. We find the most </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2023/02/high-performers/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">highly successful agents</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> are confident decision-makers, self-motivated, cool under pressure, and good at networking. </span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">Final Thoughts</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">There are many benefits to being an Independent Freight Agent. It is a career that is easy to get started and you can work from anywhere while enjoying being your own boss. You’ll have the freedom to set your own schedule and the potential to finally unlock financial freedom. The sky is literally the limit for Freight Agents. </span></p>
