<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">How do you define success? Is it </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2023/01/increase-profit-margins/"><b>increased profit margins</b></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">? Enjoying the work you do? Freedom to be there for your kids? Whatever your definition, high performers have a few traits in common. Read on to learn more about how to be a top Freight Agent.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><b>Confident Decision Maker</b></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As the owner of your freight agency, you have to make tough decisions and decide when to take risks. Businesses are living organisms and must change and adapt. How do you make difficult decisions? How do you innovate? Top performers make confident decisions and aren’t afraid of making a mistake when trying something new. </span></p>
<h2></h2>
<h3><b>Self-Motivated</b></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Top performers have an internal motivation to build their freight agency. They tackle challenges head-on, work hard, and strive for quality in all they do. They have an internal drive to go the extra mile and make sure their </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2020/04/freight-agent-customer-satisfaction/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">customers are satisfied</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Self-motivation comes when you have a reason for doing the work you do. When your “why” is clearly defined, you put yourself on the path of growth. </span></p>
<h2></h2>
<h3><b>Cool Under Pressure</b></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Freight Agents who crumble when things go wrong will often be in reactive mode, always picking up the pieces. In contrast, top performers stay calm, are able to assess the situation in order to find the best possible solution, and ask, “How can I make it work?” They know when to stand firm, when to adjust, and when to compromise. </span></p>
<h2></h2>
<h3><b>Networker</b></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The primary job of a Freight Agent is sales. Sales, after all, centers on relationships. You’re more likely to gain – and retain – a customer when that person likes you. Top-performing Freight Agents are personable, knowing how to treat people as individuals who matter. They have large professional and personal networks and are continually nurturing leads. Additionally, they are continually growing their support team, reaching out to colleagues for insight and general support, and are willing to pay it forward whenever they can. </span></p>
<h2></h2>
<h3><b>Final Thoughts on High Performers</b></h3>
