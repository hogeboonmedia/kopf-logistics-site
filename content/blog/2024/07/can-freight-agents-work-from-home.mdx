<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">About a third of American workers who can work from home choose to be fully remote, according to </span><a href="https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2023/03/30/about-a-third-of-us-workers-who-can-work-from-home-do-so-all-the-time/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Pew Research</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. The logistics industry is no exception to this trend. One role especially suited to working from home is that of a Freight Agent. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Let’s take a closer look at what a Freight Agent does, the benefits of working remotely, tools for success, and how to set up a home office. </span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">Understanding the Role of Freight Agents</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2024/02/what-is-a-freight-agent-everything-you-need-to-know/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Freight Agent </span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">is an independent contractor operating under the authority of a licensed and insured Freight Broker to coordinate the transportation of goods for shippers. This role is essential in the shipping industry as they directly manage the operational aspects of freight movement. </span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Responsibilities </span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Freight Agents arrange the pickup, transit, and delivery of cargo. They must</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Find shippers (who need goods moved) and carriers (who will move the goods)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Negotiate rates</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Manage logistics details</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ensure timely delivery</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Maintain communication with both shippers and carriers</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Provide exceptional customer service</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">It is common to interchange the roles of Freight Agent and Freight Broker, however they are actually different.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Distinctions Between Brokers and Agents</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">One of the main differences is the legal and financial requirements for </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2024/04/what-is-a-freight-broker/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Freight Brokers</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Freight Brokers have strict insurance and bonding requirements. In order to operate in the United States, Brokers are legally required to be licensed by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration </span><a href="https://www.fmcsa.dot.gov/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">(FMCSA)</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and secure a surety bond.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In contrast, Agents work on commission under the authority of a licensed Freight Broker to coordinate freight shipments. The Freight Broker, then, is responsible for the financial aspects such as invoicing shippers, paying carriers, extending credit, paying Freight Agents, and assisting with claims.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Are Freight Agents in Demand?</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">With more businesses emphasizing supply chain efficiency to ensure streamlined operations than ever before, the </span><a href="https://www.transflo.com/blog/outlook-for-freight-brokerages-2023/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">demand</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> for Freight Agents, Freight Brokers and 3PL solutions remains strong in 2024. </span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">How Stressful is Being a Freight Agent?</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Working as a Freight Agent presents a unique array of stresses, such as </span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Finding shippers</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Inconsistent pricing within the market</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Carrier problems</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Communication challenges</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Overworking</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Stress left unchecked can lead to burnout and poor work-life balance. It is important to set healthy boundaries and take time to schedule your priorities.  Consider these </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2024/05/freight-agent-stress/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">stress management tips.</span></a></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">Benefits of Remote Work for Freight Agents</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Work from home has numerous advantages such as flexibility, cost savings as a result of not commuting, and the potential for improved </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2021/01/freight-agents-balance-work-home-life/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">work-life balance</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. </span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">More flexibility</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cost savings</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">No commuting</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Increased productivity</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Comfort</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Work-life balance</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">However, as with any remote position, the benefits are directly in proportion to the mindset and boundaries set by the individual. For example, flexibility in setting a schedule can be freeing to some, and to others it can feel like work never ends. It completely depends upon the individual. At Kopf, we find the benefits often outweigh the challenges.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">Essential Tools and Technologies for Remote Freight Agents</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Technology is continually improving and is one of the most important tools for Freight Agents. From load boards, communication tools, and management software, Agents have the tools they need at their fingertips no matter where they work. </span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Load Boards</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Load boards are one of the most common ways </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2024/06/how-freight-brokers-find-loads/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Freight Agents find loads</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Remote Agents can use free or paid load boards. Often brokerages provide paid subscriptions to their independently contracted Freight Agents, such as DAT and Truckstop. </span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Communication Tools</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Customer satisfaction is the highest priority for Freight Agents. Satisfied customers provide consistent loads and positive referrals. </span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Email service: Quality brokers, like Kopf, provide this for their Agents free of charge</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mobile phone and/or VoIP: VoIP is a technology that allows Agents to make phone calls with an internet connection instead of using a phone line</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Video conferencing: such as Zoom</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Management Software</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Transportation Management Software (TMS) is essential in the Logistics Industry. TMS tracks loads, manages documentation, and much more. In order to set up a home office, Freight Agents need TMS. At Kopf, we provide each of our Agents with this valuable tool to help them succeed. </span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">Building and Maintaining Client Relationships Remotely</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Whether working in a brick and mortar brokerage or under the authority of one from home, customer satisfaction matters. Agents must be skilled at building and maintaining excellent relationships with both shippers and carriers.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Utilizing Referrals</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Successful Agents leverage referrals to maintain business and </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2023/10/diversify-book-of-business/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">diversify their book of business</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Agents can find “warm” leads by reaching out to current customers and asking for referrals. This could include referrals for their suppliers, vendors, customers, or friends within their own network. Then spend time researching these leads to learn more about their business and needs.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Effective Cold Calling</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We’ve said it before and we’ll say it again, no one likes cold calling, but cold calling isn’t dead. It is a key part of a lead generation strategy and is still one of the most effective ways to facilitate initial conversations with shippers.  </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">These are our best tips for making cold calls effectively from home.</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Use our best </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2020/06/best-cold-call-script-for-freight-agents/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">cold call script </span></a></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ask prospects any of these </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2017/12/9-cold-call-questions-independent-freight-brokers-should-be-asking/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">9 cold call questions </span></a></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Plan what you’ll do </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2018/03/5-helpful-tips-for-independent-freight-agents-before-and-after-the-cold-call/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">before and after the cold call</span></a></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Make a list of </span><a href="https://kopflogisticsgroup.com/2020/02/freight-agent-motivation/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">ways to stay motivated</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and practice them when you need to shift out of a negative mindset to a positive one.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">It takes hard work, consistency, and dealing with lots of rejection, but don’t let it stop you from pressing on.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">Setting Up a Home Office for Freight Agents</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The thought of setting up a home office might seem overwhelming, but it doesn’t have to be. Simply start with finding a space and then improve it over time. </span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Creating an Efficient Workspace</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">One of the keys to successfully working from home is a productive home office environment. Choose a dedicated work area that will help limit distractions. It is helpful if there is some natural light nearby. Sunlight can help energize and boost productivity. So if there isn’t a window nearby, take breaks from time to time to look outside. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">An efficient workspace could be in a</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Basement</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Closet</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Spare room</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Corner of a bedroom</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">After choosing a space, Agents must make sure the space provides them easy access to the equipment they will need. </span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Necessary Equipment </span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In addition to the tools mentioned earlier, we recommend our Agents have the following equipment in their home office.</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Computer (possibly a dual monitor)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">High speed internet access</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Dedicated phone line or VoIP</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">All in one printer/fax/scanner</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This equipment is typically purchased by the Agent not the brokerage. </span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">Can Freight Agents Work From Home?</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">By now you know the answer &#8211; yes! Instead of working in the office, Freight Agents can complete their job duties remotely, either from home or another location outside of the office with internet connectivity. This provides Agents with flexibility and increased work-life balance. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">At Kopf Logistics, our Independent Freight Agents work remotely. Most work from home, but several have their own office spaces outside of home, and some even have employees of their own. With an entrepreneurial spirit, strong work ethic, and a positive mindset, Agents can thrive by working remotely. </span></p>
